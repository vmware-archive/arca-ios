Getting Started

Check out our Quick Start Guide, and the package Documentation.

After you've gotten a look at what Arca can do for you, you can check out some of our Sample Apps for examples on how to make the most of the framework.

In addition, you might find our Getting to Know our Repositories guide handy, as it describes where you can find current, development, and daily releases.

Contributing

If you've fixed a bug, or added a feature, we'd love to take a look. You can check out our Contributing Guide for full details on how to get your changes rolled into the project.

If you're new to open source, and just looking to cut your teeth, check out the issues section. If you're looking for additional guidance, we try to keep an eye on the arca tag on stackoverflow#arca
